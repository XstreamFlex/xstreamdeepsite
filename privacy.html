Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2024

1. Introduction

At Xstream Flex ("we," "our," "us"), your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://xstreamflex.com or purchase our digital web development subscription services.

By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use immediately.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you purchase a subscription or interact with us, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Billing address and payment information
  • Account credentials (username, password)
  • Project details and preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

Our systems automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, and device identifiers
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
  • Error logs and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

Service Delivery

Process payments and deploy your digital products

Security

Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

Improvement

Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services

Communication

Send updates, invoices, and support messages

4. Data Retention & Security

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Active subscription + 30 days
  • Payment Data: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Project Files: 90 days after subscription ends
  • Analytics: 26 months

Security Measures

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AWS secure servers with daily backups
  • Two-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

5. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share with trusted partners when necessary:

Payment Processors

Stripe and PayPal for secure transactions

Hosting Providers

AWS and Cloudflare for reliable infrastructure

Legal Compliance

When required by law or to protect our rights
