Integration Hell: A Real Example

Here's what the typical creator's workflow looks like:

1. Teachable (Course hosting) → Zapier → Mailchimp → ConvertKit
2. Zoom (Live sessions) → Google Drive → Dropbox (Backups)
3. Stripe (Payments) → Zapier → Google Sheets → Airtable
4. Typeform (Surveys) → Zapier → Slack → Trello

Notice anything? Every single step requires another tool. When one breaks (and they always do), your entire system crumbles.
