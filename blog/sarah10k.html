How Sarah Made $10K in 30 Days

Sarah had never sold an online course before. Using Xstream Flex's Turn Key package, she went from idea to $10,000 in revenue in just 30 days. Here's exactly how...

Nov 8, 2023 • 5 min read • Case Study

Sarah, a 28-year-old yoga teacher from Denver, had 1,200 Instagram followers, zero tech skills, and an idea for a beginner’s yoga course. Four weeks later she’d banked her first $10,000 online. Below is the exact playbook—powered by Xstream Flex’s Turn Key package—that she followed.

Week 0 – The Problem

Like most creators, Sarah was stuck in analysis-paralysis:

  • Which platform should she use? (Kajabi? Teachable? ClickFunnels?)
  • How much should she charge?
  • What if nobody buys?

She discovered Xstream Flex through a friend’s tweet and booked a call the same day.

Week 1 – Idea → Offer

During her onboarding call, the Xstream Flex strategist helped Sarah:

  • Nail down a crystal-clear promise: “30-Day Yoga Foundations for Busy Beginners”.
  • Find her MVP: 4 modules, 6 hours of content, lifetime access.
  • Price it at $97 (sweet spot for impulse buyers + high enough to feel premium).
“I thought I had to film 40+ videos. They showed me how to deliver massive value with only 12 concise lessons.”

Week 2 – Build & Funnel

Xstream Flex’s Turn Key team handled everything:

  • Branded landing page with conversion-optimized copy.
  • 3-email waitlist sequence (1,417 sign-ups in 48 h).
  • Stripe checkout + order bump ($19 warm-up routine PDF).
  • Mobile-first course portal with progress tracking.

Sarah’s only job was to record the videos on her iPhone (guided by the provided script templates).

Week 3 – Launch

Launch day stats:

  • Email list: 1,417 people
  • Cart open: Wednesday 9 AM MST
  • Price: $97 (early-bird first 48 h), then $127

Results after 72 hours:

  • 103 sales x $97 = $9,991
  • 27 order bumps x $19 = $513
  • Total revenue: $10,504

Week 4 – Scale

The Xstream Flex team immediately:

  • Set up an affiliate program (10% commission).
  • Created a $297 upsell (1-on-1 coaching call).
  • Built evergreen webinar funnel (still running).

By month-end Sarah had crossed $14,700 total and quit her part-time studio job.

Key Takeaways

  • Speed > Perfection – She launched in 21 days, not 3 months.
  • Trust the process – The Turn Key package removed 90% of the tech headaches.
  • Small list, big leverage – 1,417 targeted subscribers > 15,000 cold followers.
